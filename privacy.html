7.    Cookies:

Our Website or Mobile Application use cookies, A cookie is a small text file, which is stored on your computer or other devices in order to recognize your computer or other devices at recurring visits. The cookie may transmit information via your browser with a purpose of authenticating or identifying the computer or other devices (via e.g. the IP address) or the Users or Guest Users. Cookies may contain information such as registration data and user preferences. When a server receives a request from a computer or other device which stores a cookie from the Website or Mobile Application, the server is able to use the information stored in the cookie in combination with the information stored on the server. A cookie cannot collect information from your computer or other device and cannot carry any virus or other damaging files.
