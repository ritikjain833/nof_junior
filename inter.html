INTERNATIONAL OLYMPIADS

National Olympiad Foundation (NOF) is the only scholastic examination conducted to bring out the true talent on an international level.

Any participant can appear for the subject of his/her own choice. The paper consists of multiple choice questions.

A participant has to answer 30 questions of varied difficulty levels within a duration of 45 minutes. Students from class 1st to class 12th can participate in the examination.

The papers are designed in order to be compatible for the students of all the boards, for e.g. CBSE, ICSE, IB, different State Boards, etc.
